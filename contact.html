Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I enroll in a course?

Simply browse our course catalog, select the course you want, and click "Enroll Now". You'll have instant access to all course materials.

Can I get a refund?

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all our courses. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund.

Are certificates included?

Yes, you'll receive a certificate of completion for every course you finish. These can be shared on LinkedIn and your resume.

How long do I have access?

Once you enroll, you have lifetime access to the course content, including all future updates and additions.
